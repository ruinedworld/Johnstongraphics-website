Welcome to Johnston Graphics!

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. My name is Mike Johnston aka Ruinedworld, I am a professional artist, and graphic designer with concentrations in illustration, concept art, multimedia and web design. I am also an avid gamer both on table top as well as video games, and very active in the Fallout 4 modding community. I have worked on the development team that is actively producing Sim Settlements, Industrial revolution, and Rise of the commonwealth as well as produced my own mod Ruined homes and gardens with personally over 150,000 downloads across PC and Xbox platforms.

If you want to know more about what I do, or where I do it, take a look around my site, and if you have any questions or comments regarding my work, please feel free to contact me.
